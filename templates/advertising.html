We use Project Wonderful for advertising. It took us a long time to find an advertiser that met our needs for user privacy and had a general do-good attitude. They have an excellent privacy policy, and we're glad to have finally found them. They don't track you and they have a great relationship with open software and creative commons.

All of that being said, we still will not show you ads if you have Do Not Track set. Project Wonderful is a third party, which means that we cannot entirely control how they may or may not track you. If you do not use Do Not Track and you are concerned about this, you are advised to opt-out of advertising.

We also don't show ads on mobile because that would just suck.

How do I advertise on MediaCrush?

You can bid on ads with Project Wonderful. If you're an open-source project or you feel like you're particularly in-line with the MediaCrush way, drop us a line and tell us about yourself. We'll consider showing some ads for you for free.

I hate ads but I want to support MediaCrush!

No worries! You can donate to the cause. You can also just keep on using the site with ads disabled - we won't be upset.

I run my own MediaCrush instance, how do I advertise?

To those who don't understand this question: MediaCrush is open-source. This means that any sysadmin with sufficient skill can host their own version of MediaCrush, or contribute features to ours. If you do so, and you'd like to enable advertising, please submit an application to Project Wonderful, then fill out the relevant details in config.ini. Ask about any problems you run into on the mailing list.
