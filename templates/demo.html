GifQuick

We make gifs load super fast. In fact, generally our gifs load about 10 times faster than any other image hosting site. Why? Because we convert gifs to videos when you upload them. You see, the gif file format is actually pretty ill-suited to animations. There have been huge advances in video compression since gif was first created. Specifically, GifQuick uses h.264 and theora to encode gifs as videos, with dramatic results:
